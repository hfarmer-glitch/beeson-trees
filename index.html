About Beeson Trees

Trusted Tree Surgeons in the Heart of the Cotswolds

We are a dedicated team of professional arborists committed to delivering exceptional tree care services throughout the Cotswolds and surrounding areas.

Led by Tom Beeson, our team brings over 20 years of combined experience in the arboricultural industry. We take immense pride in our work, treating every project with the same level of care and attention, whether it's a single tree pruning or a complete site clearance.

Based in the beautiful Cotswolds, we understand the importance of preserving the natural character of this stunning region. Our approach combines traditional craftsmanship with modern techniques and equipment to deliver results that enhance your property while respecting the environment.

Our Services

Comprehensive Tree & Garden Solutions

From routine maintenance to complex tree removals, we provide a full range of professional services tailored to your needs.

Tree Surgery

Expert tree pruning, crown reduction, deadwooding, and shaping. We use industry-leading techniques to promote healthy growth while enhancing the appearance of your trees.

  • Crown reduction & thinning
  • Deadwood removal
  • Formative pruning
  • Pollarding

Tree Removal

Safe and efficient removal of trees of any size, including those in confined spaces or near buildings. We employ sectional dismantling techniques for precision work.

  • Complete tree removal
  • Sectional felling
  • Stump grinding
  • Site clearance

Hedge Work

Professional hedge trimming, reduction, and maintenance to keep your boundaries looking pristine. We work with all hedge types and sizes.

  • Hedge trimming
  • Hedge reduction
  • Hedge removal
  • New hedge planting

Stump Grinding

Complete stump removal using professional grinding equipment. We remove stumps of any size, leaving your garden ready for replanting or landscaping.

  • All stump sizes
  • Root grinding
  • Site clearance
  • Waste removal

Emergency Call-Out

24/7 emergency response for storm damage, fallen trees, and dangerous limbs. We're here when you need us most.
